About Me

I am a CIF postdoctoral researcher in the Computer Science department at the Virginia Tech. working with Dr. Doug Bowman in the 3D User Interactions Group. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science at the University of Minnesota and my M.S. in Computer Science at the University of Southern California under the advisement of Dr. Evan Suma Rosenberg. My B.S. is in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Minnesota Duluth under the advisementof Dr. Stan Burns. My research interests include Human Computer Interaction, Virtual and Augmented Reality, Virtual Locomotion, Redirected Walking, and Collaborative Virtual Environments. I will soon be seeking a tenure-track faculty position to begin once my postdoc is finished.
